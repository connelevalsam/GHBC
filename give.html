We know it changes lives—ours and the people we’re reaching. At GHBC, taking action on your generosity is simple.

Tithing is our foundation of giving.

The Bible teaches us a baseline standard for giving: the tithe and on the curses and blessings. When we give the first 10% of our income to the church, we put God first in our lives. Tithing is an act of worship that expresses our gratitude, faith, and love for others. The Three-Month Tithe Challenge can help you get started.
