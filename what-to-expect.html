What to expect at Great Harvest Bible Church?

At GHBC, you'll be welcomed into a friendly, casual environment by people who are excited to see you. We want you to have an idea of what to expect when you arrive at a GHBC. All you have to do from here is come as you are and find out more for yourself!

At a weekend service, you can expect:

  • Each service to last three hours.
  • Thought-provoking and engaging messages based on the Bible.
  • Video teaching from Senior Pastor Apostle Elias Emmanue or a GHBC pastor.
  • Soul-out worship music.
  • Fun teaching for your kids.

Find your personal next step including:

  • A place to belong. LifeGroups are the people you can grow, laugh, and serve with. We have groups for men, women, couples, students, hobbyists, athletes, and more!
  • A chance to make a difference. God created you with gifts and skills that you can use to change lives in your community and the world. Serve at Life.Church or connect with one of our LifeMissions partners to get started.
  • A way to grow. We want to equip you with the tools to develop as a fully devoted follower of Christ. As an individual, family, or part of a LifeGroup, you can use resources.life.church to keep learning.

Your family will enjoy:

  • A place just for kids. Each weekend LifeKids, newborns through 6th graders, learn the truth about who God is and who they are because of Him through age-specific lessons, small groups, and trusted adult leadership.
  • A smooth transition. The Loop features worship, discussion, and relevant teaching so 5th and 6th graders can make the move from elementary to middle school.
  • A night just for teens. Switch meets on Wednesdays for students 6th through 12th grade. Loaded with fast-paced worship and interactive activities, it addresses real issues teens face each day.

Your whole family is invited.

We’re one church meeting in one location. Moms, dads, and children of every age can find something to love about Great Harvest Bible Church!
