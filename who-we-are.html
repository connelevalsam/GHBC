Great Harvest is a family of Christians that are diverse in age, talent, and background. Nevertheless, we are united in spirit, brotherly love, and common goals with expectation of the joy and peace to be found in Jesus Christ, now and for eternity. We work to take His message to all those who seek Him and desire to build their spiritual life around Him.

Mission

Great Harvest Bible Church is founded on missionary motivated goal of plundering hell and populating heaven for calvary sake. It is otherwise called “operations PHPH”. We believe and pursue the exaltation of Christ and the extension of His kingdom as the formula for being fulfilled and having access to other blessings that pertain to life and godliness.

Vision

  • We are committed to preaching the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to the world.
  • We are committed to promoting the unity of the body of Christ.
  • We engage in the training of disciples for effective ministry.
  • Publication of Christian books, magazines, tracts and other such materials necessary for the propagation of the gospel.
  • We are committed to the furtherance of the kingdom mandate through evangelistic and missionary activities in any part of Nigeria or overseas

Your Life

Wherever you are in life, you matter and you have a purpose. We want to help you become the person God has created you to be. Your journey has a next step and we'll help you find it. It could start with serving, finding God's vision for your life, giving, or joining us regularly. You don't have to take the next step alone. With a strong community of friends, you can laugh, grow, and serve with people who genuinely care about you. Find a small group, or LifeGroup, to join you on your journey.

Your Family

Strong families start with solid foundations. That's why we partner with parents to help raise the next generation to become followers of Christ. Your children and teens have opportunities throughout the week to grow relationships with each other and with God.

Our community and world

We are passionate about serving the global community and demonstrating God's love to others. Whether in person or online, individually or in groups, within our church and around the world, we are focused on supporting and engaging in relationships that provide relief and restoration to a hurting world.
